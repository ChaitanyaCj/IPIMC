About Us

POL-IMPACT Management Consultants is a political consulting firm that assists political leaders in winning elections. We provide our candidates with every big and small input under one umbrella to ensure they are represented at their best. We are specialized in Political Communications, Constituency Profiling and Strategy, Campaign Management, Social Movement, Political Impact Management, Digital and Social Media Strategy, and Polling. For reaching these specializations, we have experts from various domains for strategizing and administering the planning at multiple levels. We stand only on one belief: Politics requires courage and is not for everyone, but if you dare to get into politics, we can sustain you there

Our Mission

Our mission is to make visionary politicians heard, and their work acknowledged. We believe the work speaks volumes for the leader; Our mission at Pol-Impact is to make sure that it reaches even the last man standing in the row. We go by the saying of Kin Hubbard- "We would all like to vote for the best man, but he is never a candidate." And we pledge to bring forth that best man.

Political Research and Analytics

Political surveys and research studies play a pivotal role in building successful political campaigns. It helps to focus on the different parts of the election accurately and advances candidates' understanding of political narratives, voters' attitudes, and perceptions of their constituencies.

Pol-Impact believes in collecting the data from the grassroots and provide effective analysis to the clients. Hence, we offer you,

  • Micro-level Ward/ Booth Analysis
  • Constituency Big Data Analysis
  • Opposition research
  • Manifesto Compilation
  • Content and speech inputs
  • Candidates Identification
Political Branding and Marketing

Marketing is a powerful and unique way to unite people for a common belief, cause, or ideology. It has an incredible power to merge voices, brand identity, brand purpose, and brand image to unify the masses.

Whereas, Brand Building is all about re-discovering the overall purpose of your brand. It is about how you can make yourself more relevant to the present day and be effective in communication. So, Pol-Impact provides you

  • Candidates brand building
  • PR and Media Management
  • Political Communication Strategy
  • Social Media marketing and outreach (All platforms)
  • Content and Creation Strategy
