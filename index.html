About Us

PIMC is a political consulting firm that assists political leaders in winning elections. We provide our candidates with every big and small input under one umbrella to ensure they are represented at their best. We are specialized in Political Communications, Constituency Profiling and Strategy, Campaign Management, Social Movement, Political Impact Management, Digital and Social Media Strategy, and Polling. For reaching these specializations, we have experts from various domains for strategizing and administering the planning at multiple levels. We stand only on one belief: Politics requires courage and is not for everyone, but if you dare to get into politics, we can sustain you there.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make visionary politicians heard, and their work acknowledged. We believe the work speaks volumes for the leader; Our mission at PIMC is to make sure that it reaches even the last man standing in the row.

Our Vision

As a political consulting firm, our vision is evident- our candidate's win. However, not only win. Whether you are fighting an election, ruling the government, or in opposition, we aim to stay with you, contributing towards each of your visions.

Political Research and Analytics

Political surveys and research studies play a pivotal role in building successful political campaigns. It helps to focus on the different parts of the election accurately and advances candidates understanding of political narratives, voters' attitudes, and perceptions of their constituencies.

PIMC believes in collecting the data from the grassroots and provide effective analysis to the clients. Hence, we offer you,

  • Micro-level Ward/ Booth Analysis
  • Political Intelligence gathering
  • Constituency Big Data Analysis
  • Opposition research
  • Manifesto Compilation
  • Content and speech inputs
  • Candidates Identification
Political Branding and Marketing

Marketing is a powerful and unique way to unite people for a common belief, cause, or ideology. It has an incredible power to merge voices, brand identity, brand purpose, and brand image to unify the masses.

Whereas, Brand Building is all about re-discovering the overall purpose of your brand. It is about how you can make yourself more relevant to the present day and be effective in communication. So, PIMC provides you

  • Candidates brand building
  • PR and Media Management
  • Communication Strategy
  • Social Media marketing and outreach (All platforms)
Field Operations

The Field Operations help in evaluating the political Cooperation and assistance in the candidates' constituencies. Moreover, it helps to identify, monitor, plan, develop and execute the campaign in the various regions.

It provides candidates with the customized solutions for diverse political geography and supports the campaigns throughout their life-cycle. Hence, we cover

  • Field Surveys
  • Party Coordination-Cadre oversight
  • Booth-cadre analysis
  • Outreach Campaign Execution
  • End to End Execution
Political Strategy

The political strategy encompasses those activities which acquire, develop, and use power (clout, influence, and credibility) to gain an advantage in conflict situations. So, considering the nature of the market, history, culture, governance, stakeholders, competitors, resources, and goals we help you with

  • Make Constituency (AC) level Strategy blueprint
  • Design Campaign Planning
  • Plan Cadre Building, Execution, and Influencer Management strategy.
